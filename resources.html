Study Tips

  • Review your notes within 24 hours of each lecture.
  • Practice problems daily rather than cramming before exams.
  • Work through examples step by step before attempting homework.
  • Form a study group to discuss difficult concepts.
  • Use office hours — professors and TAs are there to help.
